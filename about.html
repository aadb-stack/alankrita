Our Story

Born from the
Looms of India.

Alankrita · Est. Pune

A saree is not a garment.
It is a ritual.

Alankrita was founded in Pune with one quiet belief — that every drape carries a story older than the woman who wears it. We travel to the looms of Kanchipuram, Banaras and Patan to hand-select the rarest weaves, then bring them home to a studio built for stillness, silk, and the pleasure of choosing slowly.

Each piece is more than thread and zari. It is a celebration meant to be passed down — from one generation of grace to the next.

The Craft

Woven by hands
that remember.

A single Kanjeevaram can take a master weaver weeks of patience — every motif counted, every gold thread laid by memory and muscle. We work directly with weaving families, paying fairly and honouring the time true silk demands.

Nothing here is rushed. Nothing here is mass. Each saree arrives at Alankrita with the fingerprints of the loom still warm on it.

The Studio

A quiet room
in the heart of Pune.

Our studio on the 5th floor of the Desai Bandhu Ambewale Building is designed to let the silk speak. Soft light, unhurried curation, and a cup of chai — choosing your saree should feel like an occasion in itself.
